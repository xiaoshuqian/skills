  • Phase splitter and Hilbert Transform:
    • Phase splitter convert a real signal into a complex analytic signal whose spectrum is 0 at negative freq.
    • freq response of the phase splitter: u(F) = (1 + jH(F))/2, where H(F) = -j*sign(F). Note H(F) is conjugate even and thus the output of the phase splitter can be written as (s(t) + j*ŝ(t))/2, where s(t) is the real valued input to the phase splitter and ŝ(t) is the Hilbert Transform of s(t).
