      • 2.4GHz radio travels almost twice as far as 5GHz radio. Halving Tx power for 2.4 GHz can get the two bands to approximately the same range. This can be used to avoid congestion in 2.4GHz band.
      • Noise floor: below -90dBm. CCA = Clear channel assessment: Device will backoff from transmitting if it detects 802.11 energy >4dB or any other energy >20 dB above noise floor.
