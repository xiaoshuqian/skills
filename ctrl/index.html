      • Total response = Free response that satisfies the init conditions + Forced response
      • Steady-state response = a particular solution that satifies the zero init conditions
      • Impuse/Step/Ramp response = a particular solution that satifies the zero init conditions when the RHS of the eq is a impulse/step/ramp function.
      • Weighting function = impulse response = a free solution that satifies the zero init conditions except for the unit nth deriviative init condition, where n is the order of the system.
